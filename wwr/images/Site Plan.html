Overview

Purpose

To show the opportunities and experiences that the audience can live in the Grijalva River,
located in Chiapa de Corzo, Mexico. Invite them to enjoy beautiful landscapes, nature, observe animals in their natural habitat and practice different activities in a river full of life. Inform the geographical characteristics of the river as well as its length. The time it takes to go through it, the depth, the type of surface under the river in the shallowest parts. What is and is not allowed to do. The type of boats appropriate for the experience, size and suitable materials. The types of kayaks, boats and the appropriate and safe number of crew members for the rapids area are also exposed. Since this activity is one of the most recurring and fun, it exposes to the different techniques for rapids.

A wide visual content is offered through photographs taken by professionals as well as by participants so that they can fall in love with a beautiful place, want to visit it and return as much as possible, inviting others to visit it.

Audience

Adventurers.
Nature lovers over 21 years old without heart or motor problems willing to enjoy unique, fun and incomparable experiences in the best of Chiapa de Corzo, the Grijalva River.
With an stable income that let them travel to the tourist area located in the state of Chiapas, Mexico. The practice of rapids in this river is not suitable for children or minors.

People over 55 years of age must have optimal health characteristics that do not imply a risk for themselves or for the rest of the crew, since the emergency stations are spaced every 1,800 meters.‚Äù This activity is not recommended for those who suffer from dizziness or sea phobia.
